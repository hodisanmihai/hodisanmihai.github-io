EXPERIENCE
My first contact with web development was back in 2020 when I wrote a small paragraph in Notepad. To be honest, I didn’t like it. A few months later, I gave it another try, but once again, it didn’t click for me. It wasn’t until the summer of 2023 that everything changed—I woke up one day, opened my laptop, and decided to dive back into web development, almost as if it came from nowhere. I started with a course on Codecademy, and that’s when my passion truly began to grow. As I progressed, I became fascinated by the world of Web Development, Design, and UI/UX. What began as a simple curiosity soon turned into a deep desire to learn, explore, and create. Since then, I’ve developed a passion for building websites that are not just functional, but also aesthetically pleasing and user-centered. The process of combining creativity with problem-solving is something I find incredibly fulfilling, and it drives me to continuously push my limits as a developer. Now, I’m committed to discovering new technologies, enhancing my skills, and developing amazing websites that leave a lasting impression.
I’ve never been one to enjoy learning purely from an academic perspective. The idea of studying things that didn’t genuinely interest me never appealed to me. In high school, I pursued an economic specialization that didn’t align with my true passions. After graduating, I enrolled in college to study Mechanical Engineering, which had always fascinated me. However, I soon realized that I didn’t want to build a career in that field. While still on this path, I had the chance to observe a Full Stack Developer writing code, and I was captivated! This experience sparked my curiosity, leading me to explore programming and the various paths it offered. After that summer when I started learning to code, I enrolled in a boot camp called Fastrack-IT, where I immersed myself in Full Stack Development for seven months. After the summer of 2023, I made the decision to drop out of my first college. This choice allowed me to fully dedicate myself to learning and developing new skills. I am now in my first year at I.E.T.I. (Oradea), excited about the journey ahead in the tech industry.
